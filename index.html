How to Play

Connect your towers to attack enemy towers or support your own.

Click on your existing connections to remove them. Removing a connection counts as a move.

You have 5 seconds to undo an accidental connection removal by clicking 'Undo' or pressing Ctrl+Z (Cmd+Z on Mac).

Earn points by capturing towers. The first to conquer all towers wins.

Each tower type has unique abilities.

Use the "Legend" button to show or hide the game legend.
